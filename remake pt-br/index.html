Associação Desportiva Confiança – Campanha na série C de 2019

I. Contexto e Preparação

O Confiança vivia uma expectativa de mudança: depois de várias temporadas na Série C, o clube almejava retornar à divisão de acesso, ou seja, à Série B.
Sob o comando do técnico Daniel Paulista, o time buscou profissionalização e aproveitou o ano para se estruturar visando esse objetivo. O início do campeonato não foi tranquilo: o Confiança enfrentou oscilações logo nas primeiras rodadas, o que exigiu paciência e ajustes.

III. Quartas de Final – O jogo que deu o acesso

Nas quartas de final, o Confiança enfrentou o Ypiranga-RS. No jogo de ida, em casa, o dragão venceu por 1 a 0.

If the video doesn’t load, click here to watch it directly on YouTube.

In the second leg, in Erechim (RS), a 1-1 draw was enough to secure promotion to Série B 2020. The result was historic — Confiança returned to the national second division after 28 years away. Fans celebrated across Sergipe, recognizing it as one of the most important achievements in the club’s modern history.

IV. Semifinal and Farewell to Série C

With promotion already guaranteed, Confiança went on to face Sampaio Corrêa in the semifinals. However, the team lost 2-0 at home and 1-0 away, ending their campaign before the final.

Image by: GloboEsporte/se

Despite the elimination, the season’s main goal — promotion — had already been accomplished.

V. Highlights and Significance of the Campaign

The 2019 journey was defined by perseverance, fan passion, and a clear focus on a single objective. Players, management, and coach Daniel Paulista emphasized that competing in Série B represented an unprecedented milestone in the club’s modern era. The campaign also boosted Confiança’s visibility, strengthened its finances, and helped the club prepare institutionally for the tougher challenges of the second division.
