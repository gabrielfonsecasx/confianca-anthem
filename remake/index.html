Associação Desportiva Confiança – 2019 Série C Campaign

I. Context and Preparation

Confiança entered the 2019 season with high expectations. After several years competing in Série C, the club aimed to finally reach the national second division. Under coach Daniel Paulista, the team focused on professionalism and structure to achieve this goal.
The beginning of the tournament wasn’t easy — Confiança struggled with inconsistency in the first rounds, forcing the coaching staff to make tactical adjustments and strengthen the team’s mentality.

Confiança was placed in Group A of the 2019 Série C. The main goal was to finish among the top four to qualify for the knockout stage. Despite a few early defeats, the team reacted strongly. A key turning point came with an important 3-1 away win against Treze, which boosted confidence. At the Batistão Stadium in Aracaju, the team also enjoyed solid fan support, with one of the best attendances of the competition that year. By maintaining consistency and securing important draws and victories, Confiança managed to qualify among the top four teams in their group, advancing to the quarterfinals.
